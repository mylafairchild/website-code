Sand and Surf Island Vacations

I used the hierarchical organization for my website. The website isn't too big and just a few pages, which keeps it within three clicks as recommended, and works really well in this type of organization.

  1. I chose a very simple and common text
  2. I used the image gallery on the home page so that I could draw in visitors with picturesque islands they could visit.
  3. I chose a complimentary color for the links and a white for hover. I chose not to do a visited color, only because I thought the site was small enough and the information split up enough that they wouldn't need to keep track of where they have visited.
  4. I designed the logo to be not too big and not to small, and to frame the beginning of each page as a presentation. I chose a web safe font color that matched the actual logo image.
  5. I made my shadow box thin so that it was subtle.
  6. I chose to make my content area thinner and longer so it was more like a flyer.
